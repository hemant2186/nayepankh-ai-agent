General Guidance:
- Students and freshers can apply.
- The internship is remote and suitable for candidates who need flexible working hours.
- Candidates should choose a role that matches their existing skills, learning goals, and interests.
- The assistant should never guarantee selection, paid compensation, or perks beyond the listed knowledge base.
- For final application deadlines, joining details, or official policy changes, candidates should contact the NayePankh Foundation team.

Role Matching Guide:
- AI Agent Development: Good for candidates with LLMs, prompt engineering, Python, APIs, chatbots, RAG, or automation interest.
- Artificial Intelligence: Good for candidates interested in AI concepts, NLP, computer vision, and intelligent systems.
- Machine Learning: Good for candidates with Python, statistics, pandas, scikit-learn, model training, and data projects.
- Python Development: Good for candidates with Python, scripting, automation, Flask, Django, or FastAPI.
- Backend Development: Good for candidates with APIs, databases, server-side logic, authentication, or deployment.
- Data Analytics: Good for candidates with SQL, Excel, dashboards, data visualization, Power BI, Tableau, or reporting.
- Full Stack Development: Good for candidates with both frontend and backend skills.
- Front End Development: Good for candidates with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and responsive UI skills.
- Web Development: Good for candidates who want to build websites and web applications.
- UI/UX Design: Good for candidates with Figma, wireframes, user research, prototypes, and visual design.
- Mobile App Development: Good for candidates interested in Android, iOS, Flutter, or React Native.
- Java Development: Good for candidates with Java, OOP, Spring, or backend fundamentals.
- WordPress Development: Good for candidates with CMS, Elementor, themes, plugins, and content websites.
- No Code Development: Good for candidates using tools such as Bubble, Webflow, Zapier, Airtable, or automation platforms.
- AI Web Development: Good for candidates interested in combining AI features with web applications.
